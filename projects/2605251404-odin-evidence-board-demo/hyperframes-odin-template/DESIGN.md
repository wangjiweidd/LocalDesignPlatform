## Style Prompt

Odin coaching videos should look like a hand-built Jianying teaching template: pale green paper background, bold Chinese serif typography, pinyin pronunciation labels above section titles, yellow highlighter title underlines, large evidence/material boards, red hand annotations, rough colored stamps, and one bottom narration subtitle. The visual priority is immediate comprehension, not polished dashboard UI.

## Colors

- Paper background: `#F3FFD9`
- Paper fade: `#FCFFF3`
- Ink: `#111111`
- Dark evidence block: `#050505`
- Dark material block: `#41483F`
- Sticker yellow: `#FFE178`
- Action orange: `#FF6A00`
- Mark red: `#D81718`
- Stamp blue: `#6EA1E8`
- Stamp yellow: `#F4CD3E`
- Stamp red: `#F34C34`

## Typography

- Display Chinese: `"Songti SC", "STSong", "Noto Serif SC", serif`
- Evidence/caption Chinese: `"PingFang SC", "Heiti SC", "Noto Sans SC", sans-serif`
- Use heavy Chinese serif only for section titles and hand-built sticker moments.
- Use heavy sans-serif for evidence paragraphs, labels, and bottom captions so viewers can scan them quickly.

## Layout

- Canvas: `1440x1920`.
- Top sticker zone: y 120-410.
- Section title zone: y 90-250, with optional pinyin line above the main title.
- Main evidence zone: y 300-1320.
- Bottom caption zone: y 1600-1840.
- Bottom caption is the only subtitle layer.
- Evidence scenes use cream paper boards with badges, rows, red arrows, and red circles.
- Category scenes use black keyword bars with mint or orange text.
- Material scenes use one media placeholder plus one document/screenshot placeholder.

## What NOT to Do

- Do not use 1080x1920 for this template.
- Do not use small dashboard cards.
- Do not add secondary explanation rows above the subtitle.
- Do not use clean SaaS fonts as the primary style.
- Do not use soft UI shadows or modern product-card aesthetics.
- Do not make the material area look like a generic SaaS dashboard.
- Do not stack full paragraph blocks on dark cards unless the reference material itself is dark.
